Subject: Mailbox synchronisation notice

Hello,

This message is to inform you that your mailbox account experienced a synchronisation issue and several incoming messages could not be delivered successfully.

The synchronisation process was unsuccessful because the mailbox could not be validated at the time of the attempt.

You may review your mailbox to retrieve pending messages and restore normal operation.

This notification applies to the Microsoft account associated with this email address.

Thank you,
Microsoft
