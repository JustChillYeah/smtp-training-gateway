Subject: Messages on hold due to security update

Dear Member,

Due to a recent system update, you are required to update and verify your mailbox.

If this action is not completed, your pending messages may not be delivered and your account may be suspended.

To avoid interruption to your email service, you must confirm your account information as soon as possible.

Please complete the required update to restore normal access.

Thank you for your cooperation,

Mail Support Team
