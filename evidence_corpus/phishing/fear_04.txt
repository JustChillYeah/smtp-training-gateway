Subject: Important notice regarding account security

Dear Customer,

We are writing to inform you that a recent security incident resulted in unauthorised access to a number of customer accounts and associated data.

Although the issue has now been resolved, some account information may have been exposed during the incident.

As a precaution, customers are being asked to review their account details and complete a security check to ensure that no further unauthorised activity has occurred.

Failure to take appropriate steps may place your account at risk of misuse.
