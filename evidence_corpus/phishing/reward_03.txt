Subject: Loyalty reward available for long-term customers

Dear Customer,

As a valued long-term customer, you have been selected to receive a loyalty reward as part of a customer appreciation programme.

You are eligible to purchase a discounted reward card for 9 GBP, which provides a credit value of 100 GBP that can be used at participating retail locations.

This offer is intended to reward continued customer loyalty and is available to eligible recipients for a limited period.

To receive your discount card and associated credit, you are required to complete the claim process as outlined.
