Subject: Final Reminder: Restriction on your account

Dear Customer,

A recent charge on your account has been disputed and requires immediate attention.

Due to this issue, we are currently unable to process any orders on your account.

To remove this restriction, you must access your account and resolve the problem as soon as possible.

Failure to take action will result in continued limitations on your account services.

Please do not reply to this message.
