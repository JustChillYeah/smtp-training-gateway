Subject: Suspicious activity detected in your account

Dear Customer,

We have detected illegal access attempts to your account, which has caused some account features to be temporarily restricted for security reasons.

Details of the suspicious activity have been recorded, including the location and time of access.

If this activity was not authorised by you, your account may be at risk of further misuse.

To resolve this issue and restore full access, you are required to review your account activity and confirm your information.
