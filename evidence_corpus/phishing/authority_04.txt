Subject: HMRC online security update

Dear Customer,

In accordance with financial regulatory requirements, HM Revenue & Customs is implementing an additional layer of security to protect taxpayer information.

As part of this process, you are required to update your account details in order to continue using online services.

This update supports compliance with regulatory standards and ensures the continued protection of tax-related information.

The update process will take only a short amount of time to complete.

Yours sincerely,
Security Advisor
HM Revenue & Customs
