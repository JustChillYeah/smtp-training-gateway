Subject: Identity theft notice regarding your account

Dear Customer,

We have identified discrepancies in information previously reported on your account and have confirmed indicators consistent with identity theft.

As a result, a protective flag has been placed on your account to prevent further misuse.

To resolve this issue and restore normal account processing, you are required to review and update your information.

Failure to address this matter may delay or prevent access to account-related services and funds.
